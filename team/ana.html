About Ana

Ana brings a high-performance perspective to Athlete Mindset. Having played at an elite level, she understands the fine line between physical readiness and mental sharpness.

Ana has spent 10 years on the soccer field, understanding the mental pressure that comes with competitive sports. Her mission is to help the next generation of athletes find joy in the grind.
