Fuel Like a Pro

You can’t drive a Ferrari on low-quality fuel. We teach young athletes that what they eat is just as important as how they train. Our workshops are practical, fun, and designed for busy student-athlete schedules.

What We Cover:

  • The Pre-Game Meal: What to eat for sustained energy without feeling heavy.
  • Recovery Window: How to refuel within 30 minutes of a game to prevent injuries.
  • Hydration Strategy: Moving beyond just water to maintain peak focus and muscle function.
  • Healthy Snack Prep: Easy ideas for between school and training.
